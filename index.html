Zeng, Chaoliang will join Huawei Kunpeng as a researcher on high-speed datacenter networking. Chaoliang received his Ph.D. degree in 2023 from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), supervised by Prof. Kai Chen. His research interests are datacenter networking, hardware acceleration, and machine learning systems.

Before joining HKUST, Chaoliang received his B.S. degree with Outstanding Graduate Award in 2018 from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC).
